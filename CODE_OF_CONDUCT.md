# Code of Conduct

Link2Context is a small open-source project for local-first content processing.
Contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, specific, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume good faith, but ask for evidence when claims affect implementation or safety.
- Keep issues and pull requests scoped to the project.
- Do not post private cookies, tokens, downloaded private content, or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's secrets, private content, or identifying information.
- Using the project to bypass platform access controls or automate abusive activity.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block participants, or decline contributions that violate these expectations.
Security-sensitive reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
